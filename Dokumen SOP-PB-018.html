## TUJUAN

Prosedur ini bertujuan untuk menstandarisasikan proses pengembangan dan pengelolaan relasi supplier guna memastikan:

Terbangunnya hubungan kemitraan yang saling menguntungkan dengan supplier
Peningkatan kapabilitas dan kinerja supplier secara berkelanjutan
Optimalisasi value chain melalui kolaborasi dan inovasi bersama supplier
Pengurangan risiko supply chain melalui pengembangan kapasitas supplier
Pencapaian competitive advantage melalui strategic supplier partnership

Penting: Program pengembangan supplier yang efektif dapat meningkatkan kinerja supply chain hingga 35% dan menghasilkan cost savings hingga 20% melalui collaborative improvement.

## RUANG LINGKUP

Prosedur ini mencakup seluruh aktivitas pengembangan dan pengelolaan relasi dengan berbagai kategori supplier:

Strategic Partners

Supplier dengan dampak bisnis tinggi dan hubungan jangka panjang

Growth Partners

Supplier dengan potensi pengembangan dan ekspansi bisnis

Core Suppliers

Supplier untuk produk inti dengan volume tinggi

Innovation Partners

Supplier dengan kemampuan inovasi dan teknologi tinggi

Penanggung Jawab:

Supplier Relationship Director: Strategic direction dan oversight
Supplier Development Manager: Program planning dan execution
Category Managers: Segment-specific development initiatives
Quality Development Team: Technical capability enhancement
Supply Chain Analysts: Performance monitoring dan improvement tracking
Innovation Coordinators: Collaborative R&D initiatives
## DEFINISI

Strategic Alliance

Kemitraan strategis dengan sharing risiko dan keuntungan

Capability Development

Program peningkatan kapabilitas teknis dan operasional supplier

Joint Innovation

Kolaborasi inovasi produk dan proses dengan supplier

Value Engineering

Optimasi value chain melalui collaborative cost reduction
## ALUR PROSES PENGEMBANGAN DAN RELASI SUPPLIER
1
Supplier Segmentation dan Profiling

Mengelompokkan supplier berdasarkan strategic importance dan development potential

Segmentation criteria: Spend volume, criticality, innovation capability, relationship maturity, growth potential

2
Gap Analysis dan Needs Assessment

Mengidentifikasi kesenjangan kapabilitas dan kebutuhan pengembangan

  • Technical capability assessment
  • Quality system evaluation
  • Operational efficiency analysis
  • Innovation potential mapping
  • Business maturity review
3
Development Roadmap Planning

Menyusun peta jalan pengembangan yang disesuaikan dengan kategori supplier

Roadmap components: Development objectives, initiatives timeline, resource allocation, success metrics, governance structure

4
Joint Goal Setting dan Alignment

Menetapkan tujuan bersama dan menyelaraskan ekspektasi dengan supplier

Alignment areas: Business objectives, performance targets, innovation agenda, risk management, value creation

5
Implementation of Development Initiatives

Melaksanakan program pengembangan sesuai roadmap yang telah disepakati

  • Technical training dan knowledge transfer
  • Process improvement projects
  • Quality system enhancement
  • Technology adoption support
  • Capacity building programs
6
Collaborative Innovation Projects

Melaksanakan proyek inovasi bersama untuk menciptakan value yang unik

Innovation focus areas: Product development, process innovation, cost optimization, sustainability initiatives, digital transformation

7
Performance Monitoring dan Review

Memantau kemajuan dan melakukan review berkala terhadap program pengembangan

Review mechanisms: Quarterly business reviews, performance dashboards, milestone tracking, benefit realization analysis

8
Relationship Maturity Assessment

Menilai tingkat kematangan hubungan dan mengidentifikasi area pengembangan selanjutnya

Maturity dimensions: Strategic alignment, operational integration, innovation collaboration, risk sharing, value co-creation

## STRATEGI DAN PROGRAM PENGEMBANGAN

Program pengembangan yang disesuaikan dengan tingkat kematangan hubungan supplier:

Tingkat Kematangan Focus Area Development Programs Expected Outcomes
Transactional Basic Compliance Quality Training, Process Standardization Consistent Quality, On-time Delivery
Cooperative Operational Excellence Lean Training, Cost Reduction Projects Cost Efficiency, Process Improvement
Collaborative Value Creation Joint Planning, Technology Transfer Innovation, Market Advantage
Strategic Business Integration Co-development, Risk Sharing Competitive Advantage, Growth
Transformational Ecosystem Leadership Industry Leadership, Digital Integration Market Leadership, Disruption

Investment Allocation by Supplier Tier:

  • Strategic Partners (Tier 1): 50% of development budget - Deep collaboration dan co-investment
  • Preferred Partners (Tier 2): 30% of development budget - Targeted capability enhancement
  • Approved Suppliers (Tier 3): 15% of development budget - Basic compliance dan quality improvement
  • Emerging Suppliers (Tier 4): 5% of development budget - Foundational training dan assessment

Key Development Initiatives:

Technical Excellence Program: Quality system certification, technical training, equipment upgrade support

Operational Improvement Program: Lean manufacturing, process optimization, digital transformation

Innovation Partnership Program: Joint R&D, new product development, technology scouting

Sustainability Development Program: Green manufacturing, carbon footprint reduction, circular economy

Digital Capability Program: Supply chain integration, data analytics, IoT implementation
