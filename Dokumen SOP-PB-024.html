## TUJUAN

Prosedur ini bertujuan untuk menstandarisasikan proses pelaporan dan analisis kinerja departemen pembelian guna memastikan:

Pengukuran yang objektif dan konsisten terhadap kinerja aktivitas pembelian
Identifikasi area improvement dan peluang penghematan biaya
Transparansi dalam pengambilan keputusan strategis procurement
Akuntabilitas terhadap target dan objectives yang telah ditetapkan
Continuous improvement melalui monitoring dan evaluasi berkala

Penting: Pelaporan kinerja yang efektif dapat meningkatkan efisiensi pembelian hingga 25% dan mengidentifikasi peluang penghematan hingga 15% dari total spend melalui data-driven analysis.

## RUANG LINGKUP

Prosedur ini mencakup seluruh aspek pelaporan dan analisis kinerja untuk berbagai dimensi performa pembelian:

Cost Performance

Analisis penghematan biaya dan efisiensi anggaran

Operational Efficiency

Pengukuran efisiensi proses dan cycle time

Quality Performance

Evaluasi kualitas supplier dan produk yang dibeli

Strategic Impact

Analisis dampak strategis aktivitas procurement

Penanggung Jawab:

Procurement Analytics Manager: Overall reporting strategy dan quality assurance
Performance Analyst: Data analysis dan report preparation
Purchasing Managers: Department performance review dan action planning
Data Specialist: Data extraction, validation, dan dashboard maintenance
Finance Business Partner: Cost analysis dan budget performance review
Senior Management: Strategic decision making berdasarkan laporan kinerja

## ALUR PROSES PELAPORAN DAN ANALISIS KINERJA PEMBELIAN
1
Data Collection dan Integration

Mengumpulkan dan mengintegrasikan data dari berbagai sistem sumber

Data sources: ERP system, supplier databases, financial systems, quality records, inventory systems

2
Data Validation dan Cleansing

Memvalidasi dan membersihkan data untuk memastikan akurasi analisis

  • Data accuracy verification
  • Completeness check
  • Consistency validation
  • Duplicate removal
  • Format standardization
3
KPI Calculation dan Performance Measurement

Menghitung Key Performance Indicators sesuai standar yang ditetapkan

Calculation methodology: Standard formulas, weighted scoring, trend analysis, benchmarking, target comparison

4
Trend Analysis dan Pattern Recognition

Menganalisis tren dan pola kinerja untuk identifikasi improvement opportunities

Analysis techniques: Time series analysis, correlation analysis, root cause analysis, predictive modeling, scenario analysis

5
Benchmarking dan Gap Analysis

Membandingkan kinerja dengan standar industri dan menganalisis kesenjangan

  • Industry benchmark comparison
  • Internal target gap analysis
  • Best practice assessment
  • Competitive analysis
  • Performance gap prioritization
6
Report Generation dan Visualization

Membuat laporan dengan visualisasi data yang efektif dan mudah dipahami

Visualization tools: Interactive dashboards, scorecards, trend charts, heat maps, comparative graphs, executive summaries

7
Management Review dan Presentation

Mempresentasikan hasil analisis kepada management untuk pengambilan keputusan

Presentation components: Executive summary, key findings, recommendations, action plan, success metrics, risk assessment

8
Action Planning dan Performance Tracking

Menyusun rencana aksi improvement dan memantau implementasinya

Tracking mechanism: Action plan development, responsibility assignment, progress monitoring, impact measurement, continuous improvement cycle

## METRIK KINERJA DAN TARGET PERFORMANCE

Key Performance Indicators utama untuk mengukur kinerja departemen pembelian:

Kategori KPI Metrik Target Benchmark
Cost Efficiency Cost Savings vs Budget ≥ 8% Industry: 5-7%
Operational Excellence Purchase Cycle Time ≤ 3 days Industry: 5 days
Supplier Performance Supplier On-time Delivery ≥ 95% Industry: 90%
Quality Management First Pass Yield Rate ≥ 98% Industry: 95%
Process Efficiency e-Procurement Adoption ≥ 85% Industry: 70%
Strategic Impact Strategic Supplier Ratio ≥ 40% Industry: 30%

Performance Rating Scale:

  • Excellent (91-100): Exceed targets significantly, industry best practice
  • Good (81-90): Meet or exceed most targets, above industry average
  • Satisfactory (71-80): Meet basic targets, at industry average
  • Needs Improvement (61-70): Below targets, requires action plan
  • Unsatisfactory (≤ 60): Significant performance gaps, urgent intervention needed

Reporting Frequency dan Audience:

Daily Reports: Operational metrics untuk purchasing staff dan supervisors

Weekly Dashboards: Department performance untuk purchasing managers

Monthly Scorecards: Comprehensive KPI review untuk department heads

Quarterly Business Reviews: Strategic performance analysis untuk senior management

Annual Performance Reports: Overall assessment untuk board of directors
